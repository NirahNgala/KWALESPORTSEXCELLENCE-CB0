Featured Programs

Sports Tourism

We host beach rugby tournaments that attract teams from across Kenya, combining sports and tourism to boost local economies and showcase Kwale’s beautiful coastline.

Twalenga Women in Sports

Dedicated to empowering women through sports leadership and participation. We provide platforms and training that amplify women's voices in athletics.

Sports Safety Campaign

We advocate for safety in sports through education, injury prevention programs, and provision of essential safety gear for young athletes.

Youth Development

We run regular training camps, mentorship sessions, and competitions that nurture talent and instill discipline among young sports enthusiasts.
